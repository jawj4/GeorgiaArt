BIOGRAPHY


I have always had a deep interest in film and photography and have acted on this passion throughout my education, from short film projects in secondary school, to taking Filmmaking at the University of West England for my degree.

In my photography I like to look for small details around me and my subjects, to bring out emotion from what I can.

I have a deep love for cinema from a diverse range of countries and time periods. My four favourite movies are Mulholland Drive (2001), The Holy Mountain (1973), Meshes of the Afternoon (1943), and I Saw the TV Glow (2024).
